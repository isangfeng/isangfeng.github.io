## About this site

A long time ago, I wanted to find a place to write down my thoughts. First, it is convenient for me to look back and sigh at how I've grown. Second, my memory is not very good — after a while, I can't remember how I dealt with a problem. Write it down, which is convenient for memory and reference. Third, some things are wrong paths I've taken — if writing them down can help others with the same problem, that's a good thing.

In my opinion, making something exist in the world that didn't exist before is called creation. Creation can be big or small. Taking a photo is creation, writing a paragraph is creation, making a video is creation, and finding a novel way to describe something is also creation. I hope this space becomes a place that wraps up my "creations".
