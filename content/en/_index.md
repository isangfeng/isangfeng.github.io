This is Feng's homepage.

A long time ago, I wanted to find a place to write down my nonsense. First, it is convenient for me to look back and sigh how childish I was at the beginning. Second, my memory is really not very good. If you encounter the same problem after a while, you can't remember how you dealt with it at all. Write it down, which is also convenient for your own memory and viewing. Third, some things are also some wrong paths that I have taken when doing things. It is also a valuable thing to write down if it can help other people who have the same problem.

In my opinion, the process of making things that do not exist in the world is called creation. Creation can be big or small. Taking a photo is called creation, writing a paragraph is called creation, making a video is also called creation, and finding a novel description is also called creation. A rainy day last summer, I would go home after buying vegetables. I saw raindrops hitting the water and splashing small splashes by a pudge. At that time, I thought that it was also very romantic to call rain flowers. Although such a creation is insignificant, it is a pity to forget it. I hope this homepage can become a space to wrap up my "creation".

The idea of making my own homepage originated from seeing the homepage of [Xie Yihui](https://yihui.org/) a long time ago. Looking at what he writes, you can feel his enthusiasm for life, which greatly inspired me. In addition, the theme of this web page is modified from [yihui/hugo-ivy](https://github.com/yihui/hugo-ivy). Thanks to him.
